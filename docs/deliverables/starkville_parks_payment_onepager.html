1. Host Advertising Partnership
As a current MCTV venue host, Starkville Parks & Recreation receives advertising across all 30 Starkville screens at the preferred discounted host rate shown above. This add-on does not change the underlying venue hosting agreement.
2. Content Ownership
All creative MCTV produces for this partnership belongs to the Host Advertiser and may be used on social media, web, print, or any medium at no additional cost. MCTV retains a non-exclusive right to display the content on its network during the term.
3. Term & Renewal
Begins on the Start Date below and continues for the agreed term (minimum 6 months). Unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least 30 days before term end, the agreement auto-renews for successive equal periods at the then-current discounted rate.
4. Payment Terms
Invoices are issued monthly on the first business day of each month; payment is due within 30 days. Accounts more than 45 days past due may have paid ad display suspended. A late fee of 1.5% per month may apply to overdue balances.
5. Content Guidelines
All content must comply with local, state, and federal law. MCTV may refuse or remove content that is offensive, misleading, or inappropriate for host venues, and will promptly notify the Host Advertiser and allow revised creative.
6. Cancellation
Either party may cancel with 60 days written notice. Cancelling this advertising add-on does not affect the underlying hosting agreement. If cancelled before term end, any prepay bonuses or discounts already applied will be prorated and billed.
7. Liability Limitation
MCTV’s total liability shall not exceed fees paid in the prior 12 months. MCTV is not liable for incidental, consequential, or indirect damages and makes no guarantee of specific business results from ad display.
